Kebab

Description

Juicy kebab that will make your mouth scream thank you!

Make this home made kebab to impress all your relatives with your cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Paprika
  • Kebab
  • Bread
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut all the vegetables
  2. Heat a frying pan
  3. Fry the kebab until golden
  4. Add the kebab, vegetables and sauce to the bread
  5. Enjoy!
