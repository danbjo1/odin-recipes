Pizza

Description

A super delcious cheese pizza with a home made marina sauce.

Invite your firends when you make this recipe because this will be amazing!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, yeast to a dough without lumps
  2. Put the tomatoes and the basil in a mixer and blend to smooth
  3. After letting the dough rest for 2 hours, bake it out to desired size
  4. Put the marinara sacue on the pizza and add the mozzarella
  5. Put the pizza in the oven on 225 degrees celsius until the cheese is golden
